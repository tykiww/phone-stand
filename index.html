Materials

1. Square/Origami paper (~8in by 8in)

2. Your very own smartphone

3. Mad folding skills!

Click > below to begin!

Step 1

Make a crease, splitting the paper horizontally, and open the paper back up (hot-dog style).

Step 2

Fold the outside edges in until they meet together at the previously created crease. Make sure to fold down the edges.

Step 3

Turn the paper around and fold the top edge to match the bottom edge, creating a crease in the middle. Make sure to fold down the edges.

Step 4

Unfold the previous crease, and fold the outside edges in until they meet together (as in step 2). Flatten the edges and unfold.

Step 6

Take one end of the paper and take the two corners and fold back at the crease, making a pointed top. Unfold the paper to create two diagonal triangles at the top.

Step 7

Holding the two ends, pucker the top and bottom creases and fold it into the middle.

Step 8

Open the opposite inside flap while holding the puckered triangle. Fold the corner in according to the crease.

Step 9

Press the corner into the triangle center.

Step 10

Fold the flat side of paper in half.

Step 11
